Manual massage 40 euro

Massage improves blood circulation, causes better oxygenation and nourishment of tissues. Improves muscle efficiency and the rate of their regeneration. In patients with neurological disorders, massage improves deep sensation by simulating nerve conduction. Massage is always performed in a position that is safe for the dog, most often on the ground, providing the dog with a comfortable position on a soft surface. It is important that the massage is performed in a quiet environment, without distractions for the animal. The owner should be present during the massage to provide the dog with maximum psychological comfort.
